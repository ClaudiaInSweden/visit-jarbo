Take a well-deserved rest from your everyday life and explore the beautiful nature and wildlife of Järbo, just about 2,5 hours north of Stockholm

Why visit

Take a mountainbike tour down Kungsberget, take a ride to one of the many lakes and catch some fish to grill in the evening.

Take a hiking tour through the astonishing Swedish forest, pick some mushrooms and berries and round off the day with a swim at Hälldammen.

Enjoy the peace in the forest and watch deers and mooses. Listen to the sounds of nature. Can you hear the busy woodpecker?

Why not book a night at Wild Nordic and watch bears, foxes, badgers or goshawks and buzzards from a hide?
